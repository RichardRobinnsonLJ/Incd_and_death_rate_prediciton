PROBLEM STATEMENT


Cancer is a leading cause of death worldwide, and understanding the factors that contribute to cancer mortality and incidence rates is crucial for effective prevention and intervention strategies.




PROBLEM SOLUTION


This project aims to develop a machine learning model that can classify different regions or populations based on their cancer mortality and incidence rates.




NEED OF THIS WEBSITE


The integration of machine learning techniques, comprehensive feature sets, customization of classification categories, consideration of temporal aspects, and adaptability to evolving knowledge and data collection methods.
